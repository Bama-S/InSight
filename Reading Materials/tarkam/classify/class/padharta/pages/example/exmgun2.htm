Examples in guNa

Now let's see some examples in guna.

Flower

Flowers are distinctly identified by their color and smell.

Food

Food is primarily identified by its taste. But it also possesses smell as one of its characteristics.

Burning wood

Burning wood is identified primarily by the heat it produces. It also has a distinct color - reddish orange;  It produces sound due to presence of water content in it and its smell can be easily identified.

Sand

Sand is identified by its smell.   Its roughness can be felt.  It is quantifiable, but is not countable.

Compounds

Compounds are formed by the conjunction of two or more elements.

Water droplets and the lotus plant

Water droplets not sticking to the leaf of the lotus plant is separateness.

Milestones

Milestones typically represent the proximity or remoteness between the source and the destination  i.e. how close or far , two places are.

Weighing machine

It is used to measure the heaviness of objects.

Living beings

They are made of body (earth), soul and mind. So they inherit all the qualities of earth, soul and mind. The quality of soul is buddhi or knowledge, i.e. ability to reason things out.  They are also characterised by quality of mind such as happiness, sorrow, good, bad, desire,etc.
