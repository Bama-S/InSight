Substances and Qualities they possess.

We have already taken a look at the 9 substances and 24 qualities. Now let us find out which substances possess what qualities.

The following five Qualities are present in all the substances(Dravya's).

  • Quantity (parimaaNa)
  • Conjunction (samyoga)
  • Disjunction (vibhaga)
  • Distinctness (prthaktva) and
  • Number (sankhya)

1. Earth    (prthvi)

Earth or 'prthvi', is characterised by it's smell. Apart from this it also has the qualities of  color ,taste and touch.Fluidity,Remoteness,Proximity and Heaviness are also present in Earth.

2. Water    (aapa)

Water is that which is cold to touch. It  also has the Qualities of taste and color. Remoteness,Proximity,Heaviness,Fluidity and Stickiness are also found in water.

3. Light    (tejas)

Light or 'tejas' is that which is hot to touch. It can be seen and felt as well.
Remoteness,Proximity,Fluidity are also present in Light.

4. Air    (vaayu)

Vaayu in sanskrit refers to air. It is that which can be felt but can't be seen.  Remoteness and Proximity are present in Air.

5. Space    (aakaasha)

Space or 'aakaasha' is that which has only the property of sound.
No other Qualities are found in Space.

6 .Time (kaala)

Time or 'kaala' is a substrate for all substances.It is all pervading.

7. Direction (Dik)

Direction referred to as 'dik' is also all pervading.

8. Soul (Aatmaa)

Soul or 'aatmaa' is the substrate of knowledge.
Remoteness,Proximity,Knowledge are Qualities present in Soul.

9. Mind (Manas)

The mind is the instrument for experiencing emotions.  The qualities of mind are happiness, sorrow, anger, etc.
