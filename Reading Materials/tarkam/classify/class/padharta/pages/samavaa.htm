समवाय

Inherence

Inherence is the sixth category in the classification chart and is referred to as 'Samavaaya'
(pronounced as sa-ma-vaa-ya).
It is an inseparable eternal relation between objects.

Unlike conjunction or samyoga (discussed in guna), inherence is not a separable or removable relation, but is a permanent quality.

Samavaaya is an independent category.  'Kanaada' calls it the relation between cause and effect.

Inherence is the relationship that exists between objects and their characteristics.  It is thus one relationship existing between two things inseparably connected.

Some such relations where samavaaya is found are

Colour is inherent in a leaf.
Smell is inherent in the earth and all earthly things.
