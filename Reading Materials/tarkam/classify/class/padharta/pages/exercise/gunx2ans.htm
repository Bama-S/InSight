Answers to the questions in guna section.

1. Suns rays
Substance - Light (Primarily source of light and heat).
Quality - It is hot to touch(Property of Tejas).It can be classified into 7 different colors.

2. Sugarcane
Substance - Sugarcane is made of earth and contains water
Quality - It is characterized by its sweet taste(due to water) and characterestic smell(property of earth)

3. Flute
Substance - Flute is made of bamboo (earth).
Quality - It produces sound.

4. Stars
Substance - Light
Quality - They are sources of light(Tejas).They are innumerable in nature (numeric).

5. Volcanic Lava
Substance - Volcanic lava is liquid state of earth (substance).
Quality -  It is characterized by its heat,color and its fluidity.This fluidity is the result of the interaction of earth(rocks etc.) and fire.
