shareera-indriya-vishaya

Shareeram refers to the body.
Indriyam refers to the organ of perception.
Vishayam refers to the objects that are made of the dravya.

Earth
Body Similar to that of humans
Organ Nose (organ by which one perceives smell).
Object Clay, stone, etc.
Water
Body varuna-lokam, the clouds (think of cloud-men !!)
Organ Tongue(organ by which one perceives  taste).
Object Rivers, lakes, streams, etc.
Light
Body aaditya lokam, the sun
Organ Retina of the eye (organ by which one perceives color)
Object It is of 4 kinds: earthy, heavenly, gastric and mineral.  Earthy variety is the common fire.  Lightning can be classified as heavenly.   The gastric variety is digestive acid. Gold, platinum are classified under the mineral category due to their lustre.
Air
Body vaayu lokam, the world of wind god
Organ skin (organ by which one perceives touch)
Object the cause of movement of trees
