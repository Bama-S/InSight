Creation of Universe

The first to come into existence was the space. The only characteristic of space was sound (Perceived by the ear).
Air then came into being, in the form of various gases which could be felt (Perceived by the skin).
The above gases, due to the mutual attraction between them, came together to form various stars which were burning masses of fire, a source of light (Perceived by the eye).
Later, these stars burnt and cooled down, that led to condensation forming water, which possessed the property of taste (Perceived by the tongue).
Finally these celestial bodies exhibited qualities possess by all of the above along with additional property of smell (Perceived by the nose).
