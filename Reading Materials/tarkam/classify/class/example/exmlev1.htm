Examples in Classification

The examples mentioned here are classified under one of the seven groups.

Sweetness Quality ( it is a quality that relates to substance like sugar, honey, etc.)
Human body Substance ( it can be felt, seen, etc.)
Airplane Substance (made of matter - can be heard, seen, etc.)
Suckling Generality (common to all mammals)
Hunger Quality (It is a quality possessed by living beings)
Playing Action    (Involves an act done by a person)
Darkness Absence of light   (we shall come back to this definition later in this course)
Fruits Substance (that which can be seen, tasted, smelt. etc)
Talent Specialty of an person
(when we talk about an individual)
                  Sting of scorpion Specialty of all scorpions
(when we talk scorpions as a group)
Walking Action involves the act of movement performed by a person
Baldness Absence or loss of hair on one's head.
Heat and fire    Inherence
(Inseparable relationship between heat and fire)
Drowning Action - going down in water due to inability to swim
School    Uniform Generality (That which is supposed to be worn by all school students)
Specialty (When we talk about the school uniform of a particular school it is a specialty of the group)
