This method of drawing arrows may  look cumbersome for  larger

numbers and may infact confuse you.Here is a much easier way of doing

the same  process   without creating confusion.

Consider the multiplication of 4567  with 760.

Reverse one of  the numbers and write it below the other number

as shown below.

Now perform the multiplication of  digits that  fall on the same line.

Write the least significant digit of the product in the answer column

and keep the remaining digits as remainder.In the above case the

product 0(=7 x 0), has only one digit and is written in the answer

column.There is no remainder.

        Shift the reversed number to the left by one digit while keeping

the other number stationery .Do  the same process of multiplying the

corresponding digits.Sum the products  so obtained .

The least significant digit of the sum (2 in this stage ) forms the next  digit

of the result.The remaining digits (4 in this stage) form the remainder to be

added during the next stage.

Shift the reversed number  again by one digit to the left and perform the multiplication

and addition process.The result is added with the remainder in the previous stage.

Proceed the work till all the digits in the reversed number have crossed

the number  at the top.

Thus the result of the product 4567 x 760 is 3470920.

Yes! There is no more confusion with arrows however large the numbers may be.

After reversing one of the numbers and writing it below the other number, the method

essentially involves only four steps  that   has to be done repeatedly.

1)Multiply digits that fall in the same line

2)Add the products obtained in the previous step.This sum is added with any

previous remainder.

3)Write  the least significant digit of the sum in the result column and the rest of the

digits form the remainder for the next stage.

4)Shift the reversed number to the left by one digit.Goto step 1.

Repeat the steps until all the digits in the reversed number have crossed the other number.
