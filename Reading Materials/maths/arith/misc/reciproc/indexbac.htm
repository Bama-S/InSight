Now let's see the Indian method. Before going to that, let me tell you something about the reciprocal of a number ending with 9. The reciprocal of a 'n' digit number repeats itself after some digits. That set of repeating digits ends with 1. For (eg.) the reciprocal of 9 has only one repeating digit and that digit is 1. With this knowledge let's proceed to calculate the reciprocal of 19.
