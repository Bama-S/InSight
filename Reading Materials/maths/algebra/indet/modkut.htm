Bhaskara's modification to Aryabhatta's Kuttaka method :

Bhaskara simplified Aryabhata's kuttaka method to solve equations of type ax+c=by. This is shown by the following example.
Consider the indeterminate equation:

137x + 10 = 60y

Express the larger of the coefficients(in this case 137 > 60, so choose 137) as product of the smaller coefficient (i.e 60). The division is continued  till the last remainder is 1.

137 = 60*2 + 17
60 = 17*3 + 9
17 = 9*1 + 8
9 = 8*1 + 1
