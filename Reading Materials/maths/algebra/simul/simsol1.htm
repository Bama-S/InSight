What would mom have done ?

 

Let the amount of flour measured in a cup be x and amount of sugar measured out be y. First she measured 2 cups of flour & 1 of sugar. The cake weighed 1½ kg.

So, we have 2x + y = 1½          --- eqn A

Next she measured 3 cups of flour & 2 of sugar. The cake weighed 2½ kg.

So, 3x + 2y = 2½                      --- eqn B

eqn A * 2 gives 4x + 2y = 3      --- eqn C

eqn C - eqn B gives x = ½ kg .

Substituting in eqn A  gives y = ½ kg.
