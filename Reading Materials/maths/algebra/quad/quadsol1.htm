What time is it ?

First, let 't' be the time interval elapsed from the time of Priya's starting her drive to when she had covered 5 km.

Recollecting the equations of motion from our knowledge of Physics,we use the equation s = ut + ½ at2 , where s is the distance, u is the initial velocity , a is the acceleration and t, the time interval.

We know that s = 5 km, u = 4 kmph and a = 2 kmph2 . The equation that we now get is :

5 = 4t + ½ . (2). t2

t2 + 4t - 5 = 0

(t + 5) (t - 1) = 0

We get t = -5 & t = 1.

Since time cannot be negative, t = 1.

From this, Priya deduces that the time is 1 hour from when she left home. So, it is 7.00 am.
