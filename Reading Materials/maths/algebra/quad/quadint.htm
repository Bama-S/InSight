What are quadratic equations ?

At 6.00 am on a  fine morning, Priya decided to go out for a drive. She reversed her car from the garage and started to drive along Beach Road. Her initial velocity was 4 kmph. Then, she enforced a constant acceleration of 2 kmph2.

When she had driven 5 km, she found that she had forgotten to wear her watch. Do you think she can find out what time it is, even without looking at her watch?

It's simple ! She just has to form a quadratic equation & find out the time she had been driving.

Try it out yourself ! And, check if your answer matches with Priya's, here !!!

Mathematically speaking, quadratic equations are equations of the second degree.

For example, ax2 + bx + c = 0 is a quadratic equation. The solution to such an equation will yield two values for the unknown quantity x.
