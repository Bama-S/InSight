Solution to trigonometric equations

The solution to trigonometric equations can be achieved, using the concept of tribhujank.

Examples :

(1) cos A = 1 / sqrt(2)

Tribhujank for A is 1, ? , sqrt(2).
Hence, the solution is 1,1,sqrt(2) and 1, -1,sqrt(2), using the relation between the elements of the tribhujank.

(2) cot B = 1 / sqrt(3)

Tribhujank for B is 1, sqrt(3), ?
The 2 solutions are 1, sqrt(3), 2  and  1, sqrt(3), -2.
