Phases of the moon:

After a Poornima day, the next Amavasaya day occurs about 29-30 days after the previous one and the whole sequence of Amavasya-Poornima-Amavasaya. Through these sequences we get to see the different phases of the moon, namely :

1) Invisible,
2) Crescent,
3) Half,
4) Gibbous(more than half)
5) Full.
