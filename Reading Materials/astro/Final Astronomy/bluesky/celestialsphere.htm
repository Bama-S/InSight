The Celestial Sphere:

The dome or the hemi-sphere which we saw previously is so large that its size is as big as your mind can let you imagine. This is just one half of the total sphere and we can say that the other half is below us. This HUGE sphere is what's called the CELESTIAL SPHERE. You know that the earth is surrounded by various objects like the sun, moon, stars, etc. These celestial objects are considered to be situated on the surface of this sphere.

Our Mother Earth is more or less a sphere, somewhat similar to the celestial sphere. You should have studied in your geography lessons about the Earth's EQUATOR. This is an imaginary line constructed by the geographers around the centre of the earth. It serves as a line of reference to explain many geographic phenomena concerning the earth. Going by the same terms, the astronomers also defined an imaginary line around the celestial sphere. It may be considered as a 'logical extension' of the Earth's equator. This line of reference is defined as the circle got by the intersection of the plane of the Earth's equator and the Celestial Sphere. This circle is what the astronomers call as the CELESTIAL EQUATOR.
