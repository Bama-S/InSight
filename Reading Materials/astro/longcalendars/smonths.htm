NAMES OF THE SOLAR MONTHS (SAURA MAASAS)

 

MOST OF INDIA TAMILNADU KERALA STARTING DAY *
Vaisakha Chittirai Mesha April 14
Jyeshtha Vaikasi Vrishabha May 15
Ashadha Ani Mithuna June 15
Sravana Adi Karkataka July 17
Bhadrapada Avani Simha August 17
Ashwayuja or Ashvina Purattasi Kanya September 17
Kartika Aipasi Tula October 18
Margashira or Agrahayana Kartigai Vrishchika November 17
Pushya or Pausa Margazhi Dhanus December 16
Magha Thai Makara January 14
Phalguna Masi Kumbha February 13
Chaitra Panguni Meena March 15

* It is possible that in some years the months may start one day earlier or one day later.
