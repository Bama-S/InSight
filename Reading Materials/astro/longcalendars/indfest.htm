CRITERIA FOR FIXING SOME IMPORTANT INDIAN FESTIVALS

Unless specified otherwise, the months refer to Chandra maasas (Amanta). S stands for Sukla paksha; K for Krishna paksha. The numbers refer to tithis; for instance 5 stands for Panchami, 30 stands for Amavasya.

This table should be understood as follows. The festival Ganesha Chaturthi is determined by Bhadrapada S4, Maha Sivaratri by Magha K14 and Holikadahan Holi by Phalguna S15 the day after Holikadahan etc.
